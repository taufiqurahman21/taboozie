About Us

Taboozie is a card game and website that tackles one of parenting's most challenging dilemmas – how to talk about sex education with children. Many parents feel unprepared and uncomfortable addressing these crucial topics, often staying silent or deferring to inadequate school programs. This communication gap leaves children vulnerable to misinformation and unsafe situations. We believe that learning about these essential life topics should be an interesting and safe experience. Our mission is to transform traditionally awkward conversations into natural, engaging family moments through playful, interactive tools. Through simplicity, kindness, and care, we break down societal taboos and create a world where every child can grow up informed, empowered, and confident in themselves—one conversation at a time.
